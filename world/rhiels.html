Inferno

This is the place most humans think of when they hear "The Abyss". It is a place of lava, fires and rocks hot enough to burn your shoes of in seconds. Annaxxor is the ruler of the Inferno, and he owns several portals that are used to transport demons to the surface. This is where the demons during the demon wars came from. This is also why Anaxxor's brood is mostly seen as the stereotypical demons amongst humans.

The Depths

In the deepest part of The Abyss lies The Depths. This place is completely devoid of light and all demons here are blind or possess darkvision. Down here lies a prison, sharing the name of its creator, Raknarokk. Heretical demons, and the occasional half-demon, are thrown in its pits, and locked behind thick steel gates, only to live in a constant darkness until the day they die. Hundreds of demons are forced to live in the pits together, and the weak ones usually get stomped or eaten within minutes.

The Ruins of Pandemonium

The ruins of an ancient city once ruled by the dragons. Once their reign ended the demons that lived there began breeding, destroying and killing indiscriminately. As such, the city went to ruins, and all that remained was chaos. Oorieria was the first to try and stop the sensless chaos. In time, this made her respected by the others, and she eventually became their queen, and god. Now, the ruins of the once great city is the home for the oldest and wisest of all demons, and they have devoted their lives to protect their queen, their god, Oorieria.

The Vile Soil

This desolate place is covered in a green mist. Inhaling it would make any man sick with multiple diseases. The demons that live here are highly poision resistant. They creep underground, listeneing, waiting for their next meal. Any stray creature that wanders into their soil will soon die by sickness. Once their body starts to rot, they come, rip it apart and consume its flesh. They do it for their love for their lord, Crûdomo, the tormentor. They live for the pain he inflicts. They live for the pain of their meals.

Realm of the Depraved

The Abyssal Lord Skorma was named monarch by the leaderless demons living in this rhiel. They heard his words and saw his actions, and they were smitten by it. Here there are no rules, no leadership, no dreams and goals. Strength is everything. The strongest survive, the weak die. It is as it should be, and all who are weak welcome death with open arms. You accept your fate, and use it to let the true survivors of this world become even stronger, getting closer and closer to perfection. Only the perfect demon will stand on top at the very end of it all.
