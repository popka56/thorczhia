Known God Artifacts

God Artifacts are items created by gods, imbued with their power. These artifacts exists on a higher plane of existance, just like gods themselves, and are therefore among the only known things that can harm a deity. Long ago, the gods created many of these artifacts to reward heroes and rulers of the world. Eventually, the gods grew weary of all these powerful items, they started to look like a threat to their own power. And so, the gods began collecting them and threw them into a volcano until almost none remained.

Dastudr di Verthicha

One of the four 'Power Stones'. A red rock that gives the user incredible strength. If used correctly, the user can break mountains with just their fists.

Current location: Inside Du'Dain as a power source.

Risa

One of the four 'Power Stones'. A blue slab containing unlimited magical power. It is a perfect power source for magical contraptions. Using it would be like using magic while in the leyline convergence.

Current location: Inside Du'Dain as a power source.

Fountain of Youth

One of the four 'Power Stones'. A green crystal with the power to 'renew' anything. It can be used to heal wounds or even prevent one from aging.

Current location: Inside Du'Dain as a power source.

Angelic Abyss

One of the four 'Power Stones'. A white gem said to be able to stop 'The Reincarnation'. What this truly means is unknown.

Current location: Inside Du'Dain as a power source.

The Glass Sword of Lon Gin

A purple sword of glass made with exreme care by Lon Gin. It was a gift for a young man, whose name has forever remained in mystery.

Current location: Unknown.
