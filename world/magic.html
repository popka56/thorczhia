Magic

Magic is a mystical energy, the 'life-force', of the world. It was first discovered by alchemists of Gircish long ago. There are so called 'leylines', magical seams, that cover the world. These leylines are in constant motion but there is a point where they all converge. When using magic, you use the power of these leylines to create power through thought. Therefore, depending on where the user is in relation to these leylines, the power of that users' magic can be more or less effective. The point of convergence of the magic leylines is directly over Kirmer, which is why the Archmage Council is situated there.
