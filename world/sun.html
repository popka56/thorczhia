The Sun

The sun is a large body of eternal fire created by Meiva. It constantly circles around Thorczhia, with each lap taking approximately 24 hours. The sun is located inside the illusion of heaven and lights up the entire surface below. It is a very important symbol for many human cultures.

According to dosatsu the sun is the most powerful spirit in the physical plane and husband to the azure moon.

The people of Oqpa believes the sun to be the creator of all, their one true god, one who is always watching and judging.
