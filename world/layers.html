The World

Of Its Secrets

Surface

The surface of Thorczhia is where most of its living creatures and plantlife exists.

Wer Oplin

Far below, deep in the earth is Wer Oplin. It is a cold and dark place of dark creatues. The tempereature down here is far below freezing point, it takes only a few seconds for a human to turn into an ice statue. However, once you get closer to the Abyss the temperature will rise again.

Wer Oplin used to be completely devoid of life, but once the dwarves began digging into the mountains they eventually hit this layer. And when they did, they awoke the dark creatues that had been sleeping there since time immemorial.

The Abyss

The home of all demons. It is a huge open tunnel that serves as the layer between Wer Oplin and Wer Ssifisv. The Abyss is made of red rock and contains seas of lava, magical crystals and hordes and hordes of evil demons just waiting for their moment to return to the surface to kill all humans.

The Abyss is split into seven regions called Rhiels. Each of these Rhiels is ruled by one of the Abyssal Lords.

Wer Ssifisv

Wer Ssifisv, like Wer Oplin, is extremely cold. And this layer gets even colder as you get deeper. No humans have ever ventured into these depths, and no other beings could exist under such harsch circumstances.

The Void

The archmages of Kirmer have long studied the very center of the planet. It is large, and constantly growing. One day it will consume it all, as it destroys everything in its path. They named it The Void, and unless they find a way to stop it, it will be the end for Thorczhia, albeit in a few tens of thousands of years in the future.
