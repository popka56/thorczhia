Alchemy

Alchemy is one of the oldest forms of science in Thorczhia. It prospered for a long time in Gircish, but with the discovery of magic, it became a less and less popular practice. The veteran alchemists would dismiss magic as 'non-science' for years. Today, magic is considered to be just as real as alchemy, but alchemy itself is no longer used as much. This is mostly because you can do the same things with magic, in less time and with less precision. Alchemy is still a big part of the culture of Gircish, and an important part of history, but these days it is almost exclusively used for medicine. Anyone can keep some alchemy-made medicine at home, but few can cast a spell of healing in a dire situation.
