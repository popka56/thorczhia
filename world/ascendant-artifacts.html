Known Ascendant Artifacts

Much like God Artifacts, Ascendant Artifacts are items that exist on a higher plane of existance. But, unlike God Artifacts, Ascendant Artifacts were not originally created by a deity. These artifacts have instead ascended to this plane through great ordeal.

Dave John's Locket

The locket of the first ever pirate, Dave John. It has the power to take over the minds of others, more specifically others whose minds are weaker than the wielder.

Last seen: In the hands of the pirate captain Li'Del. Current location is unknown.

Water Cutter

A legendary sword created by the flames of a volcano. It was made to slay a famous frost dragon. It is most well known from a classic poem:

Untouched by water.
No keys lead to it.
The children speak of its tales.
It was forged to remove the dragonscales.

Current location: Unknown.

Cursed Blade of Ernil

A blade once belonging to a noble Elven family. The prince Ernil of said family commited horrible crimes which cursed his soul to be bound to the blade. He is the sword, and the blade is him. It was locked in a coffin along with his body at his death. But his soul still lingers to the blade, forever. The sword has the ability to instantly teleport anywhere that this tormented soul wishes, as well as absorbing elements and using it as its own.

Last seen: In the hands of the Shogun of Fujui, Haruki Yamamoto. It has since been lost after the war ended on Na'ak.

Staff of Amadeus

The staff of the last Elder Mage, Amadeus. It ascended through the immense power being funneled into from both Meiva and Partum, just before the battle against Bekilip.

Last seen: Taken by Drar.

Daggers of Toshi

Two daggers used by Toshi. They ascended through the immense power being funneled into from both Meiva and Partum, just before the battle against Bekilip.

Current location: Locked in the Archmage Treasury.

Greatsword of Forgal

The greatsword used by Forgal, 'The Army'. It ascended through the immense power being funneled into from both Meiva and Partum, just before the battle against Bekilip.

Last seen: Taken by Drar.

Grimoire of Toby

The magical implement used by Toby. It ascended through the immense power being funneled into from both Meiva and Partum, just before the battle against Bekilip.

Last seen: In the hands of Archmage Toby.

Quarterstaff of Marlok

A quarterstaff wielded by Marlok. It ascended through the immense power being funneled into from both Meiva and Partum, just before the battle against Bekilip.

Last seen: After Marloks' death it was lost. It is said to still be somewhere on Na'ak.

Bow of Lucan

The bow of Lucan. It ascended through the immense power being funneled into from both Meiva and Partum, just before the battle against Bekilip.

Current location: Presumed stolen by the murderer of Lucan.

Meivas' Judgement

The greataxe of Meiva, a giant axe engulfed in green flames with the ability to change its' size according to the users' wishes.

Current location: In the hands of Gerry Meiva.

Partums' Silverflame

The warpick of Partum, a huge warpick egulfed in bright white flames that can change its' size by the will of the user.

Current location: In the hands of Charles Partum.

The Nine Hidden Blades

Nine blades made by a master smith on Na'ak long ago. Each blade has an element attached to it, like Lightning or Fire. Six out of these nine blades have been cursed by countless years of fighting in the endless war of Na'ak.

Current location: Locked in the Archmage Treasury.

The Beacon of Peace

The blade of Wu Han, the shogun highly regarded as the man who ended the endless war of Na'ak. The blade ascended through the battle against the Oni, Yokohama, and countless other battles that came after.

Current location: On display in a museum in Iimasu on Na'ak.
