Fewki

The birthplace of the Human Deities. Fewki consists of thirteen islands which are constantly in motion because of the huge maelstrom surrounding the entire Luh. The Temple of Meiva is located here, and is used as a meeting place for the gods.

Kirmer

Kirmer is the point in the world where the magic leylines are the strongest. Throughout the ages Kirmer has been the Luh who has known peace the most, hence why it is also known as the Land of Peace. Yet, despite the name, Kirmer is also where some of the most brutal wars have taken place, like the three demon wars. Kirmer is also the location for the Temple of Partum.

Skriiod

Skriiod is a desolate land that used to be overrun by the living dead. Now it is the home to a large order of noble knights called the Flaming Shield. The Temple of Bekilip was first built here, but has since been sealed up the the gods.

Gircish

The birthplace of magic and alchemy. It is a place where nature is everything to its inhabitants. Back when magic and alchemy was discovered, two factions were made. The ones who believed magic would be the future of humanity moved to Kirmer, where natural magic was the strongest, while the others who believed in alchemy being humanity's future stayed here. Many exotic animals and foods come from Gircish, including Felines, who originated from here.

Oqpa

The Desert Lands of Oqpa is the home of some of the oldest human technology and beliefs. The Precursors thrived here until their eventual demise, one that they had predicted. Today the Precursor technology is used by all the people of Oqpa on a daily basis, even if it is still not entirely understood and cannot be replicated. Oqpa is also the home of the Pharao, as well as the world's most notorious pirates.

Sissaro

Sissaro is the name of the largest volcano on Thorczhia. It has been inactive ever since the Age of Man began. The volcano has been refurbished into a huge tinkertown; Sot. The city of Sot used to be one of the richest and most inflentual cities in the world. But its influence grows weaker for every passing year, and its brotherhood, the Mist has disappeared. Some say it is for the better, others disagree. Smoke and steam and ash still covers the skies above, making it seem as active of a volcano as any. It was here that the dwarves first dug too deep, and awoke the dark creatures in Wer Oplin.

Wusari

Wusari is a relatively untouched by the modern civliazations. It is great plains and thick forests filled with many thriving animals and other creatures. Most humans living here live in the hundreds of tribes around the land. However, there is one larger island outside of the archipelego which is home to more modern towns and a capital city. These people have made it a point to preserve the nature and tribes that live in the Luh.

Na'ak

The Far Eastern Kingdoms of Na'ak was under constant wars for thousands of years, so long that none live to remember what started it. The Shogun of Iikomora, Wu Han, was the one to finally bring an end to the never-ending conflict. Now, peace has reached the kingdoms and a new era for the Luh has just begun.

Neltil

Neltil is a small island far away from any calm seas. On this islans is a pillar extending all the way up to Heaven, or so it is said. No one who has tried to climb it has ever returned. The legend says the island is protected by three guardians; The Great Serpent Lykladi, World's Wisdom Nosaamur and the King of the Skies Krer.
