The World

Of Its Secrets

The Illusion of Heaven

An illusion created by Meiva, only visible during daytime. It is the blue sky you see when looking up.

Heaven

Between the illusions lies the plane where all souls of living beings and objects alike are collected after death. The archmages have observed it and tried to understand it for a really long time, but no one really knows how or why it happens.

The Illusion of Space

An illusion created by Meiva, only visible during nighttime. It is the black sky with twinkling stars you see when looking up during the night.

The Nothingness

Beyond the illusions lies an endless void. No life, no light or dark. Travelling or living out here would be impossible. Only the dragons and their gods are aware of the truth of the nothingness, and they do not feel like sharing.
