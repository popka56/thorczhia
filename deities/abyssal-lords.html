Of Its Gods

Abyssal Lords

The Abyssal Lords are of the most powerful demons in all of Thorczhia. Lesser demons consider them their gods. They each rule over a Rhiel in The Abyss. An unspoken rule between the lords is to never reign over more than one Rhiel at a time. It is a rule that has been broken many times during the ages, however most believe it is the true way still. If an Abyssal Lord is killed by another lord, that lord may pick the demon to take its place. If a lesser demon kills a lord however, then that demon will become the new Abyssal Lord.

The Lords of the Abyss

XV:i. Anaxxor, Ruler of Inferno

The big bad himself. Anaxxor is the most well-known and feared demon among humans on the surface. During the demon wars, it was Anaxxor's hordes that attacked first. Anaxxor owns several portals to the surface, and almost all demons who use them are of his own. He is very respected by all kinds of demons, and is even placed in very high regard by the other Abyssal Lords.

Raknarokk, Lord of the Depths

Deep within the pits lies Raknarokk and his demon prison by the same name. The name brings fear in demons and imps. Few humans know of him, but his prison has many legends and rumors echoing throughout the world. No one has ever seen Raknarokk's true form, except the other Abyssal Lords. Therefore, some demons question wheter or not he is just a fabrication to leave The Depths without a ruler.

Ooriera, Queen of Pandemonium

The oldest and wisest of all demons. Demons never stop growing, and Ooriera has grown so much that all that remains is a huge lump of flesh, with a thousand eyes and hundreds of horns sticking out all over her body. She is completely immobile, and is constantly protected by her loyal children. However, she still possesses poweful magicks and is able to speak through others by controlling their minds.

Crûdomo, The Tormentor

The oldest demon next to Ooriera. However, Crûdomo knew what fate awaited him if he were to live too long. He did not want to become an immobile pile of flesh. His true love is pain, a life without inflicting pain onto others would not be one worth living, according to him. So, when Crûdomo was in his prime, he killed himself. Yet his soul still remains inhabiting his dead body. Crûdomo is now just a demon skeleton clad in black robes, still inflicting pain onto his children for all eternity.

Meerusô, Empress of Flame

Also known as the Flame Demon-Witch. Meerusô has completely mastered the art of magical flames. She can manipulate and create almost any shape of fire. She and her domain is often wrapped completely in her own flaming walls and barriers, protecting it from other non-fire resistant demons.

Skorma, Monarch of the Depraved

Skorma believes life and death is one and the same. To live is to die. And dying means fulfilling your life. To Skorma, killing is an act of love, one which all his followers have accepted with open arms. Even the other Abyssal Lords find Skorma's philosopyh admirable, even if they themselves do not follow his vision. Skorma is seen as a primal demon, very dangeroues and unpredictable. His strength is bar none, even generally accepted by all Lords to be the strongest of them all.

Rû-Dôn, The Fiendish King

Rû-Dôn, the hornless demon, is often the butt of all jokes among the Abyssal Lords. He is not invited to their meetings and his name is often used to insult their own when they fail them. The reality is that Rû-Dôn is just quite mad and does not regard himself with others.
