The Holy Circle

The Holy Circle, also known as The Holy Seven (and previously known as The Holy Six), is a group of deities officially recognized by humans. They were all once normal humans themselves and achieved godhood through some incredible feat or heroic deed. As of the Third Age these gods have become even more popular than The Human Deities among the human races.

The Deities

Aru

Aru stands for family and birth. She is a god popular among large human families.

Du'Dain

The latest god to join the Holy Circle. Du'Dain stands for guidance.

Gorn

Gorn is the most popular god among Halflings. He stands for civilization and its inventions and laws.

Migri

The god of love and fertility.

Rakka

Rakka stands for luck, chance and travel.

Sor

The god of magic. Is also largely considered to be the god of arts and beauty.

Yarnar

Yarnar is often depicted as a monkey with six arms and is mostly followed by Felines. Yarnar stands for animal life, nature, light and food.
